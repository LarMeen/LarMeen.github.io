Explore Belarus

Discover the beauty of Belarus by visiting these amazing places:

Minsk

Minsk, the capital city of Belarus, is known for its historic architecture and vibrant culture.

Mir Castle

Visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Mir Castle, a stunning example of medieval architecture.

Belovezhskaya Puscha

Explore the ancient Belovezhskaya Puscha, home to unique wildlife including European bison.

Braslaw Lakes

Relax by the picturesque Braslaw Lakes, a popular destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.
