Discover Belarusian Landscape

Explore the diverse and captivating landscapes of Belarus:

Belovezhskiy Forest

Immerse yourself in the ancient beauty of the Belovezhskiy Forest, one of the last and largest remaining parts of the immense primeval forest that once stretched across the European Plain.

Belarusian Lakes

Marvel at the serene beauty of Belarusian lakes, such as Braslaw Lakes, Narach Lake, and Lake Drivyaty. These picturesque lakes are perfect for relaxation and outdoor activities.

Belovezhskaya Pushcha

Visit Belovezhskaya Pushcha, a UNESCO World Heritage Site spanning the border between Poland and Belarus. Explore its ancient woodlands and diverse wildlife.

Rolling Hills and Meadows

Witness the rolling hills and expansive meadows that characterize the Belarusian countryside, offering a tranquil and picturesque setting.
